Hi, I'm Jeff
Counselor in Las Vegas
I am a Licensed Clinical Social Worker and Therapist. Having earned my Masters in Social Work from the University of Illinois, I have worked with kids and teens, as well as adults of all backgrounds, in schools and with for-profit agencies and non-profit settings.
My goal is to help each individual reach their highest potential and live intentionally. My approach — simple yet straightforward. Whether it is easing anxieties, working through trauma or bettering yourself and relationships with those in your life, we work toward progress in your unique situation. My motto is like that of Zen Buddhism, ‘you are perfect as you are, but you could use a little improvement'. We will take it from there…
Hi, I'm Nicky
Care Coordinator in Las Vegas
I am a Licensed Medical Social Worker, having graduated from the University of Nevada-Reno (go Wolfpack!). I have helped countless individuals, families and children in social services for years, and more specifically within the Las Vegas community.
My goal is solution-focused care that reflects a holistic approach, one that is supportive and empathic, focusing on your individual goals toward a better self and more meaningful life experience.
