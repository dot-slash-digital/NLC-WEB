Hi, I'm Jeff
Counselor in Las Vegas
I’m a Licensed Clinical Social Worker, Coach, Therapist, Husband and Dad. I earned my Masters in Social Work from the University of Illinois-Urbana, and have been working to help people of all different backgrounds for over 20 years.
My goal is to help you to become the best version of you. I believe that best version of you is inside of you – we just need to shine a light on it. My approach is to do this work together - you bring yourself and your desire to grow, and I bring my life and professional experiences.
My motto is like the Zen Buddhist maxim – ‘you’re perfect as you, but you could use a little improvement.’ Here to help.
Hi, I'm Nicky
Care Coordinator in Las Vegas
I am a licensed social worker, having achieved a Master of Social Work degree from the University of Nevada-Reno (go Wolfpack!). My career includes helping people and organizations in social services for over a decade.
My philosophy is solution-focused, and my objective is to coordinate care that reflects a holistic approach, and one that is supportive and empathic. I find meaning from being a mother, wife, helper, and advocate.
It is my true passion to help you focus on your individualistic and unique strengths, and take your life to the next level that you envision.
